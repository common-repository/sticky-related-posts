== Description ==
<p>Increase pageviews and website CTR, display your <strong>related posts with thumbnails</strong> in a modern sticky style. Best way to show similar posts inside your WordPress post that will sure increase the content visibility inside your webpage.</p>

<h3>Sticky Related Posts WordPress Plugin</h3>
<blockquote><strong>Sticky Related Posts</strong> is an advanced and modern style WordPress related posts plugin that you can easily color customize based on your website layout and theme styles.</blockquote>

<h4>Sticky Related Posts Demo</h4>
<blockquote><a href="http://processsteps.coolplugins.net/sticky-related-posts">Sticky Related Posts Demo</a></blockquote>

<h3>Plugin Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Display related posts inside sticky footer.</li>
<li>Related posts based on category or tags.</li>
<li>Option to hide or display thumbnails.</li>
<li>Custom settings for fonts and background colors.</li>
<li>Choose number of related posts to display.</li>
<li>Clean user friendly and modern design(based on SCSS).</li>
<li>Increase customer engagement and website pageviews</li>
<li>Best seo friendly related posts plugin.</li>
<li>Order related posts based on name, date, author etc.</li>
</ul>
